If agile is about building software in tiny increments, how can we see a big picture to design the software ?

Answer:

In an agile team, the big picture evolves along with the software. With each iteration, the team improves the design of the system so that it is as good as it can be for the system

Instead of trying to build the infrastructure to support the future requirements, we focus on the current structure of the system, making it as good as it can be.



How to know a software design is poor ?

Answer:

Rigidity - The design is hard to change

Fragility - The design is easy to break

Immobility - The design is hard to reuse

Viscosity - It is hard to do the right thing

Needless Complexity - Overdesign

Needless Repetition - Mouse abuse

Opacity - Disorganized expression



How to know and evaluate a design is hard to change ?

A design is hard to change if a single change causes a cascade of subsequent changes in dependent modules

When take a change in a system, developers need to estimate for that change. But later, they work through the change, they realize they need to change hug portions of the codes, modify far modules than they had estimated.



How to know and evaluate a design is easy to break ?

When a single change is made, the program is broken in many places.

When the developers try to fix those problems, that leads to even more problems.

When a change is made, that also makes unexpected problems.



How to know and evaluate a design is hard to reuse ?

When the system contains parts that could be useful in other systems



How to know and evaluate a design is needless repedition ?

The design contains repeating structures that could be unified under a single abstraction.

But the effort and risk involved with separating those parts from the original system are too high.



How to know and evaluate a design is hard to do the right thing (Viscosity) ?

A viscous project is a project in which the design of the software is hard to preserve

Viscosity has two forms:

Viscosity of the software

When faced with a change, the developers have many ways to make that change.

Some ways keep the design and some way do not.

If the ways which keep the design harder to do than rest. That is the viscosity of the software.

Viscosity of the environment

Viscosity of environment comes about when the development environment is slow and inefficient
