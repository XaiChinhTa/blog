What is software design ?

The UML diagrams may represent parts of design, but it is not the design

The design of a software project is an abstract concept

The design can be represented by many different media, but its final embodiment is source code (the detailed shape and structure of each module, class, and method)



The signals of the bad design

Rigidity - The design is hard to change

Fragility - The design is easy to break

Immobility - The design is hard to reuse

Viscosity - It is hard to do the right thing

Needless Complexity

Opacity - Disorganized expression

Needless Repetition

When the code repeated to many times, the developers may miss an abstraction.

When one of the repetitions exists bugs, you need to find and fix all the repetitions.

However, since each repetition is slightly different from every other, the fix is not always the same


The root cause of stimulating the software to Rot

Some requirement changes need to be made quickly, and they may be made by the developers who are not familar with original design.

Through the changes, the original was violated bit by bit.

But we knew the requirements always change so if our designs are failed


How agile solve the problem

An agile team do not work with the initial design. Rather, they keep the design of the system as clean and simple as possiple and back it up with a lot of unit tests.

And after each iteration, the team improves the design continuously.

Questions

Question 1: If agile is about building software in tiny increments, how can we see a big picture to design the software ?

In an agile team, the big picture evolves along with the software. With each iteration, the team improves the design of the system so that it is as good as it can be for the system

Instead of trying to build the infrastructure to support the future requirements, we focus on the current structure of the system, making it as good as it can be.


Question 2: How to know and evaluate a design is hard to change ?

A design is hard to change if a single change causes a cascade of subsequent changes in dependent modules

When take a change in a system, developers need to estimate for that change. But later, they work through the change, they realize they need to change hug portions of the codes, modify far modules than they had estimated.


Question 3: How to know and evaluate a design is easy to break ?

When a single change is made, the program is broken in many places.

When the developers try to fix those problems, that leads to even more problems.

When a change is made, that also makes unexpected problems.


Question 4: How to know and evaluate a design is hard to reuse ?

When the system contains parts that could be useful in other systems


Question 5: How to know and evaluate a design is needless repedition ?

The design contains repeating structures that could be unified under a single abstraction.

But the effort and risk involved with separating those parts from the original system are too high.


Question 6: How to know and evaluate a design is hard to do the right thing (Viscosity) ?

A viscous project is a project in which the design of the software is hard to preserve

Viscosity has two forms:

Viscosity of the software

When faced with a change, the developers have many ways to make that change.

Some ways keep the design and some way do not.

If the ways which keep the design harder to do than rest. That is the viscosity of the software.

Viscosity of the environment

Viscosity of environment comes about when the development environment is slow and inefficient

When developers first write a module, the code may seem clear to them

Later, after they do the other things, they may return to that module and wonder how they could have written anything so awful.
